Custom GPTs

Train AI tools on your own knowledge base to automate support, generate content, and scale operations intelligently.

What Are Custom GPTs?

Custom GPTs are AI models trained on your internal documents, FAQs, product data, and procedures to deliver expert-level answers or create content instantly — tailored exactly to your business needs.

What You Can Do

  • Provide 24/7 customer support trained on your real documentation
  • Create internal AI assistants for employee onboarding or task help
  • Generate reports, emails, and summaries on command
  • Integrate GPTs into websites, CRMs, or Slack

How We Build It

  • Step 1: We collect training materials — knowledge bases, SOPs, documents
  • Step 2: We design a GPT system prompt and optionally fine-tune a model
  • Step 3: We build a front-end tool (chatbot, dashboard, etc.) and test functionality
  • Step 4: We deploy it and monitor it for quality control

Real Client Use Cases

  • Real Estate Company: A GPT bot that answered 90% of tenant questions before escalation
  • Law Firm: Internal GPT trained on case law and contracts for fast referencing
  • Ecom Brand: Used GPTs to write product descriptions and email sequences
